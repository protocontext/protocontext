## section: About

Grand Hotel Riviera is located on the scenic Amalfi Coast in southern Italy.
4-star boutique hotel with 42 unique rooms and suites.
Rooftop pool, private beach access, and two on-site restaurants.
Family-owned since 1962.

## section: Rooms and Prices

Classic Room: from €120/night
Superior Sea View: from €180/night
Junior Suite: from €250/night
Penthouse Suite: from €400/night
All rooms include Mediterranean breakfast, wifi, and air conditioning.
Minimum stay 2 nights in high season (June-September).

## section: Dining

Trattoria del Mare — Beachfront restaurant, fresh seafood daily.

## section: About

La Trattoria del Porto serves traditional Italian seafood since 1965.
Located at Via del Porto 12, directly on the waterfront.
Family-owned, third generation. Seats 60 inside, 40 on the terrace.
Featured in Gambero Rosso and Michelin Guide.

## section: Menu Highlights

Antipasti: Crudo di mare (€16), Caponata (€10), Arancini (€8)
Primi: Pasta con le sarde (€14), Spaghetti ai ricci (€18), Busiate al pesto (€13)
Secondi: Grilled swordfish (€22), Mixed fried seafood (€18), Tuna steak (€20)
Dolci: Cannoli (€7), Cassata (€8), Granita with brioche (€6)
Wine list: 80+ Italian labels. House wine €5/glass.
